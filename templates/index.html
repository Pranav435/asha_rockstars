Wait wait dude, who is Asha Savla?

Asha Savla is a dance teacher living in Bangalore, India. She trained for Bharatanatyam way back in the late 1990's, successfully passing up to the 7th exam, Visharat. She now teaches Bollywood and contemporary dance to students aged 3 to 7.
